Setting up Span tables
Print
Modified on: Wed, Nov 13, 2024 at  6:41 AM
Utilizing span tables in Smartbuild can be a huge timesaver. Once they are setup, you can put your old charts away and have Smartbuild look up the data for you.
Watch this video to learn how:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fzquHCVNmbo&t=7s
You can find free calculations online.  One example would be :
https://awc.org/calculators/span-options-calculator-for-wood-joists-and-rafters/
