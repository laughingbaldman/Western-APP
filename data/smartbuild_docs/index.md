## Platform Boundary: Paradigm ERP vs SmartBuild

- **Paradigm ERP (Paragon)** is Western's operational system of record for quotes, orders, accounting, inventory, and fulfillment.
- **SmartBuild** is a separate barn-building web application for building design and material generation.
- **Integration flow**: design in SmartBuild, then transfer/import into Paradigm for quote and order processing.
