Contractor Preferences

- Capitol Builders
- Currently onle buys lumber windows and walk doors
- likes to build with 2x4 for the girts
- likes to build with OSB716 for the roof
- if there is osb include underlayments
- likes a 43lb snow load usually
- sometimes builds with wet set brackets
- for stick builds likes to build box headers out of 2x6
- Does not want Footing Pads
- if it is a house pack will need drop gables
- they do corner bracing out of 2x4 not 2x6
- add 5% to a tight OSB material count
- They pass the lumber pricing directly through to the customer and do not charge more if job is short, they would rather have some extra to have the customer keep or buy back from the customer. This means round up a little bit on a tight count
